Risky Strats

Who are the people behind Risky strats?

Risky Strats was founded by devAdrian, currently known as hlelo_wolrd. It was than later transferred to a group now known as "White Collar Games", run by VitalWinter. This group also contained other developers that helped maintain and update the game. The developers that helped maintain it are Clv2, GameAnnounce, Stickmasterluke, DevUItra, Osyris, and hlelo_wolrd. Over the course of its (Risky Strats) long existnce Risky strats has developed a small but active community. Through this community we a few staff memebers that helped moderate the discord server and game. The said staff members are Purplescheer, Sha, TheGentleNoob, puck, ike, Mumisa, and ven.

How can I learn to play?

Lucky for you, the new version of Risky Strats will include a game tutorial, you can currently learn to play by watching the many tutorials there are on Youtube. You can always join the Discord server and ask for help from our active community. You can find a discord invite link below the Risky Strats game page on Roblox.
